Volleyball

Some facts about the game of volleyball:

  1. The game was invented in 1895 by William G. Morgan
  2. It was added as an Olympic Game in 1964 during the Tokyo Games
  3. The average player jumps around 300 times during a match
  4. The longest match ever played lasted 101 hours

You can learn more about volleyball here at USAVolleyball.org
